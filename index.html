About CartoXR

CartoXR pioneers the fusion of Geoinformatics and Extended Reality (XR). We design and deliver immersive solutions — UAV mapping, AR indoor navigation, VR visualization, and XR-enabled spatial analytics — for industry, government, and academia. Our approach combines rigorous geospatial methods with practical immersive UX to accelerate decision-making and training.
